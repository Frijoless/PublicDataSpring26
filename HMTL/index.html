Her

A short review of the movie Her

Link to Her IMDB

I want to ask two questions when looking at Spike Jonze's film Her.

1: How meaningful are Theodore’s experiences with Samantha compared with the “real” people he encounters in everyday life?
2: Are Theodore and Samantha are really in love?

1: When Theodore interacts with people he isn't all there because he's still in his head when it comes to his ex-wife and his personality makes it so he doesn't show 100% of himself to people. He comes off as shallow to his date when she thinks all he wants is sex(granted that is what he wanted when he talks to Samantha) and only seems to be there for a good time instead of cultivating a new relationship. He's can appear vulnerable to other people if he wanted to and this is shown through his letters for his job. Off the bat with Samantha he act differently because she feels human to him, but at the time he sees her as a computer. He starts to get comfortable with Samantha after going out on a couple of dates paired with phone sex and he starts to forget that she's an Operating System(OS). He only starts to pull away from her like everyone else once Catherine congratulated him on getting a "wife without dealing the challenges of dealing with anything real" and how he "can't handle real emotions." They only start to repair their relationship once he comes to term that Samantha is an OS and there is nothing wrong with that (and getting some help with that fact from Amy's OS experience).
2:There are some interesting points they touched on when it comes to relationships with OS like how dating one is a rare occurrence, how people peruse other people's OS and they're not designed to always say yes shown through someone getting rejected by their OS. The fact that it's not guaranteed means there is something special between Samantha and Theodore (I'm also comparing this to interspecies relationship in sci-fi media so I think it's a real relationship).

I recommend the movie because it's good and an interesting look at future AI from 2013
